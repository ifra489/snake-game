## **How to Play**

1. Open `index.html` in a browser.  
2. **Desktop:** Use arrow keys to move the snake.  
3. **Mobile:** Use on-screen buttons to move the snake.  
4. Press **Pause** to pause/resume the game.  
5. Eat the food to increase your score.  
6. Avoid hitting walls or your own tail!
